About Us

ISKCON Center Miraj is dedicated to spreading Krishna Consciousness through Bhagavad Gita teachings, Harinam Sankirtan, spiritual education, prasadam distribution and youth outreach.

Our Activities

Weekly Satsang

Kirtan, discourse and prasadam every week.

Gita Course

Systematic study of Bhagavad-gita As It Is.

Sankirtan

Congregational chanting and outreach.

Book Distribution

Distribution of Srila Prabhupada's books.

Food For Life

Prasadam distribution and community service.

Youth Preaching

Programs in colleges and youth forums.
