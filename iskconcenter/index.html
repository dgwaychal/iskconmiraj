About ISKCON Miraj

ISKCON Miraj is dedicated to spreading Krishna Consciousness through Bhagavad Gita teachings, Harinam Sankirtan, book distribution, prasadam distribution and youth outreach programs.

Our Activities

Weekly Satsang

Kirtan, Bhagavatam discourse and prasadam.

Bhagavad Gita Course

Systematic study of Bhagavad Gita As It Is.

Harinam Sankirtan

Congregational chanting and outreach.

Book Distribution

Distribution of Srila Prabhupada's books.

Food For Life

Free prasadam distribution and community service.

Youth Programs

Spiritual education in colleges and campuses.
